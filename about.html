About Us

About Us

"KAWISH FOUNDATION" Aurangabad, under the patronage of YASH CHARITABLE FOUNDATION, has been launching various initiatives for educational counseling, teaching, and career development since 2012.

The primary aim is to mentor and nurture talent among students from minority and other weaker sections of society. The target group includes students from Classes IX onwards up to Post Graduation, as well as parents and teachers who seek to prepare and appear for civil service and other competitive examinations


These initiatives place special emphasis on creating awareness about various higher professions through the Civil Services Examination and helping participants with early goal setting and strategizing.

To date, the foundation has successfully conducted more than 21 batches, 30 seminars, and numerous career guidance sessions and webinars.

Why Choose Us?

YASH CHARITABLE FOUNDATION (Regd.
